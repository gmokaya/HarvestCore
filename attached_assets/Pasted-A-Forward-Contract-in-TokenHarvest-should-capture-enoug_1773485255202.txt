A **Forward Contract in TokenHarvest** should capture enough information to **define the trade, secure collateral, manage delivery, and enable automated settlement**. Because it replaces exchange-regulated futures, the **data structure must be very clear and enforceable**.

Below is the **recommended forward contract data structure**.

---

# 1. Contract Identification

These fields uniquely identify the contract.

* **Contract ID** – Unique system identifier
* **Blockchain Contract Hash** – Hash recorded on IOTA/BNC
* **Contract Type** – Forward Contract
* **Contract Status** – Draft / Open / Accepted / Active / Settled / Cancelled / Defaulted
* **Creation Date**
* **Expiration Date**

---

# 2. Parties to the Contract

Defines who is buying and selling.

### Seller

* Seller ID
* Seller Name
* Organization ID (if cooperative or trader group)
* Wallet Address / Payment Account
* KYC Verification Status

### Buyer

* Buyer ID
* Buyer Name
* Organization ID
* Wallet Address / Payment Account
* KYC Verification Status

---

# 3. Commodity Details

Defines the asset being traded.

* **Commodity Type** (Maize, Beans, Coffee, etc.)
* **Commodity Grade / Quality Standard**
* **Moisture Content** (important for grains)
* **Packaging Type** (bags, bulk, pallets)
* **Total Quantity**
* **Unit of Measure** (kg, tons, bags)
* **Warehouse Receipt ID** (if already stored)

Optional but useful:

* **Certification** (organic, export grade)
* **Origin Location**

---

# 4. Pricing Terms

Defines the financial terms of the forward contract.

* **Agreed Forward Price** (per unit)
* **Total Contract Value**
* **Currency** (KES / Stablecoin)
* **AI Suggested Price** (system-generated reference)
* **Price Lock Timestamp**

Optional:

* **Price Adjustment Clause** (if tied to market index)

---

# 5. Delivery Terms

Defines how and when the commodity will be delivered.

* **Delivery Date**
* **Delivery Window** (e.g., 10–15 Sept)
* **Delivery Location** (warehouse ID or processor facility)
* **Delivery Method**

  * Warehouse pickup
  * Transport arranged by buyer
  * Platform logistics

Optional:

* **Partial Delivery Allowed (Yes/No)**

---

# 6. Collateral / Security

This protects both parties from default.

Possible collateral types:

* **Tokenized Warehouse Receipt**
* **Inventory Lock**
* **Cash Margin Deposit**
* **Loan-backed commodity**

Fields include:

* Collateral Type
* Collateral Value
* Collateral Token ID (if blockchain tokenized)
* Collateral Lock Status

---

# 7. Payment Terms

Defines how settlement will occur.

* **Payment Method**

  * M-PESA
  * Bank (PesaLink)
  * Stablecoin
* **Payment Schedule**

  * Upfront
  * On delivery
  * Partial deposit + final settlement

Optional:

* **Escrow Wallet ID**

---

# 8. Risk & Compliance Fields

Used by the **AI risk engine**.

* Buyer Risk Score
* Seller Risk Score
* Contract Risk Rating
* Insurance Coverage (if available)

---

# 9. Smart Contract / Blockchain Fields

If tokenized.

* Smart Contract Address
* Blockchain Network (IOTA / BNC)
* Tokenized Contract ID
* Settlement Trigger Rules

Example:

```
Settlement Condition:
Delivery Confirmed AND Payment Received
```

---

# 10. Contract Lifecycle Tracking

Used for monitoring.

* Contract Status Updates
* Delivery Confirmation Timestamp
* Payment Confirmation Timestamp
* Settlement Timestamp

---

# 11. Dispute Resolution

Important in private contracts.

* Dispute Status
* Arbitration Authority
* Evidence Upload (documents, inspection photos)

---

# 12. Audit & Traceability

Ensures transparency.

* Created By
* Approved By
* Last Modified
* Digital Signatures of Parties

---

# Example Simplified Contract Record

```
Contract ID: FC-2026-00012
Commodity: White Maize
Quantity: 50 Tons
Price: KES 3,800 per bag
Delivery Date: 15 September 2026
Warehouse: Kitale Grain Warehouse
Buyer: Unga Mills Ltd
Seller: Trans Nzoia Farmers Cooperative
Collateral: Tokenized Warehouse Receipt
Payment Method: M-PESA / PesaLink
Contract Status: Active
Blockchain Hash: 0x8f4c32...
```

---

# Important Feature for TokenHarvest

Because your platform includes **Tokenized Warehouse Receipts**, the forward contract should also include:

**Commodity Token Reference**

```
Token ID: TH-MAIZE-2026-009
Blockchain: IOTA
Warehouse: Kitale Grain Store
Quantity Locked: 50 Tons
```

This **prevents double selling** and enables automated settlement.
